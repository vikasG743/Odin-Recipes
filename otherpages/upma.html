Upma Recipe

step by step recipe for yummy Upma


  1. Roast Rava: Dry roast 1 cup of semolina (suji/rava) in a pan on medium heat until fragrant and crunchy, then set aside.
  2. Tempering: Heat 2 tablespoons of oil or ghee and add 1 teaspoon of mustard seeds until they splutter.
  3. Add Lentils & Nuts: Stir in 1 teaspoon each of urad dal and chana dal, plus 8–10 cashews, frying until golden brown.
  4. Sauté Aromatics: Add 1 chopped onion, 1–2 green chilies, 1 sprig of curry leaves, and 1 teaspoon of grated ginger; sauté until onions are translucent.
  5. Boil Water: Pour in 3 cups of water (for soft upma) or 2.5 cups (for fluffy upma) and add salt to taste.
  6. Combine: Once the water boils vigorously, lower the flame and add the roasted rava in a slow stream while stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  7. Simmer: Cover the pan and cook on the lowest heat for 2–4 minutes until the water is fully absorbed.
  8. Finish & Fluff: Turn off the heat, stir in a teaspoon of ghee and a squeeze of lemon juice, then let it rest covered for 5 minutes before fluffing with a fork.

Serving suggestions: Serve hot with Coconut Chutney or a side of Lemon Pickle.
